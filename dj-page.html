DJ Reims

Bio:

DJ Reims was born and bred in the west suburbs of Chicago. His music background comes from being an avid gamer at a young age and has loved the compositions of video games since then. His idea of wearing a mask came from wanting to have a memorable image/presence, while obtaining a personal life as well.

Artists that have influenced me:

  • Dubstep: Excision, Zomboy, JPhelpz, Barely Alive, and Virtual Riot
  • Electro House: Wolfgang Gartner, Mord Fustang, and Deadmau5
  • House: Disclosure, Phlegmatic Dogs, Daft Punk, and Tchami

About Me:

I am a local DJ available for bookings in Chicago and the surrounding area/states. I mainly mix tracks produced by other various artists/producers and I also produce my own music and continue to grow both as a DJ and as a musician.
